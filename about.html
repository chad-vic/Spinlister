How It Works

Spinlister helps active people connect with trusted bike owners all over the world

Spinlister is a premier bike rental and sharing service that provides a platform to connect bike owners with active people looking to rent or borrow around the world. Through our service, owners list their bikes, renters search for a bike in whatever area they want, and complete the rental process easily through our website, mobile app, or in-store kiosks. We even offer various protection options and delivery services to make everyone happy.

How We Build Trust

done

ID Verification

Renters and listers verify their Spinlister accounts with their Credit Card, mobile phone number, Twitter and Facebook. The verification validates your online identity to help people get a better idea of who they're renting to or from. Verifying your account helps others trust you and makes your ride show up higher in search results.

star

Community Reviews

We welcome all of our users to review each other following their rental to provide a resource that highlights everyone’s experiences on Spinlister. These reviews help create a sense of community for our users and build a marketplace based on safety and trust

lock

Keep Your Privacy

We never share your information with anyone until you confirm their rental. You can keep all communications in our secure, private messaging system so you don't need to share your contact info and you can choose where you'd like to meet renters if you'd rather not share your address.
